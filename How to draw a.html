How to Draw a Heart Shaped Gift Box Easy Step by Step for Kids

May 22, 2020 Drawing Tutorial Category: Valentine's Day

Valentine's Day is a festival about love, romance, flowers, chocolates, and greeting cards. On this day, lovers will give each other gifts to express love.

A step-by-step tutorial that teaches you how to draw this heart-shaped gift box for Valentine's Day. If kids like it, let them try following the steps below!

1. Draw a big love heart first. You can also design a gift box in other shape.

2.Draw two lines horizontally, and then draw two lines vertically.

3.Let's draw a big bow at the intersection of these four lines.

4.Finally, simply color it and the beautiful Valentine's Day gift is done!
